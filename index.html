Meet Our Chef

KELVIN CHEUNG
Kelvin Cheung is best known for three things: his meeting and mating of techniques and traditions creating diverse yet distinct cuisine, the perfect Sunday brunch sometimes featuring his over the top #KelCakes for Bollywood stars, and his advocacy for wellness - for his farmers, diners, and the planet.

Saransh Goila
Saransh Goila is an Indian chef who won Food Food Maha Challenge. He is the founder of a popular Mumbai-based restaurant 'Goila Butter Chicken' and author of the acclaimed food travelogue 'India on my Platter'.
